Get to know the Hybrids

Innovation

Yamaha hybrid pianos strike the perfect balance between our tradition of old-world craftsmanship and our unwavering commitment to new technology. Today, Yamaha innovations such as Disklavier, SILENT and TransAcoustic have helped redefine what a piano can do. So artists are free to make music like you’ve never heard before.

Solutions

Whether you perform in the world’s most prestigious concert halls, a recording studio or studio apartment, the touch, tone and beauty of a Yamaha should be available to all of us. Our hybrids are created to bring the iconic sound of a Yamaha piano to more people in more places than ever before.

Integration

Where does the acoustic piano end and the digital piano begin? Yamaha hybrids blur the line between digital and acoustic with proprietary technologies that work together to emulate the natural feeling and sound of our acoustic pianos. From the resonance of the soundboard, rim and frame to the nuanced release of the keys.
