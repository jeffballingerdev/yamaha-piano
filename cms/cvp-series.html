Clavinova CVP

The most entertaining Clavinova ever.

Our flagship CVP is everything you’d expect from a Clavinova. And more. Bring in virtual session musicians. Harmonize with backup singers. Add authentic voices to hundreds of digital instruments. An intuitive color touchscreen controls a new world of entertaining possibilities. You won’t believe how it sounds. Or what it can do.

Features

  • The world’s finest concert grands at your fingertips.
  • Grand touch.
  • Authentic acoustic resonance.
  • You’re the band leader.
  • Piano Room with Session Mode.
  • Bring your own backup singers.
  • Let the lights be your guide.
  • Truly authentic keyboard voices.
  • Intuitive color touchscreen.
  • Save your settings.
  • Record and share any way you want.
  • Keys the other digital pianos can’t touch.
  • Our advanced binaural recording process recreates the tone, power and nuanced colors of our flagship Yamaha CFX and Bösendorfer Imperial concert grands. The resulting sound is so natural you’ll almost forget you’re playing a digital piano.
  • Weighted, graded keys reproduce the authentic touch, response and natural key return of a grand piano while our Smooth Release Technology models the subtle interactions of the piano strings with the damper.
  • Two proprietary technologies work together to emulate the natural feeling of an acoustic piano. Our innovative Virtual Resonance Modeling (VRM) recreates the resonance of the soundboard, rim and frame. Our Tactile Response System (TRS) reproduces the subtle sensation you feel in your hands and feet while pressing the keys and pedals. Even when playing with headphones.
  • Accompaniment Styles lets you add ensembles of virtual musicians to your performances simply by playing chords in real time. Add any one of hundreds of musical genres, from small jazz ensembles to full orchestras. You control the chords, harmony and dynamic and the ensembles follow you. Developed by professional session musicians, it’s the perfect accompaniment for any style of music you can imagine.
  • Add up to three session musicians into Piano Room to accompany you as you play in 40 different musical styles. Session musicians follow your harmonies and volume in full-keyboard or split mode and you can record each session with the touch of a button. Piano Room also includes the flagship Yamaha CFX, Bösendorfer Grand Pianos and vintage Electric Pianos.
  • Vocal Harmony lets you plug in a microphone and sing along with three virtual background vocalists. Vocal Harmony2 allows you to correct the pitch, level, number of harmonies and more.
  • Follow Lights help intuitively guide your fingers through some of your favorite songs. Choose a song in our Follow Lights Library and the recording plays along with you at your speed. You’ll be playing the right notes at the right time, in no time.
  • Yamaha Super Articulation 2 Voices (SA2) adds natural sounds including fret noise and body knocks from guitars and inhales, exhales and glissandos from wind instruments to your performance. All with a simple touch of the keys or pedals.
  • Touch, slide, swipe or rotate this easy-to-read color screen for quick access to your piano’s state-of-the-art features.
  • Save and recall your favorite keyboard settings with the press of a button. It’s never been easier to change your sound.
  • Clavinova CVP is compatible with a variety of audio formats so everyone who’s anyone can hear your latest project.
  • Our proprietary synthetic ebony and ivory keytops have a natural, comfortable feel that players of all levels can’t wait to get their hands on.
